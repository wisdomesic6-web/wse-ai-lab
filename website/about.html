About

Builders first. From Lagos, for the continent.

WSE Lab Consult is a software studio that ships. We started because too much African software stops at the demo — and too many good businesses were left waiting for tools built for someone else's market.

WSE Lab Consult is a bootstrapped studio, funded entirely by the work we ship. No venture capital, no runway pressure — just software that has to earn its place in a real business, or it doesn't ship.

That constraint is the point. When you build with your own money for customers who pay with theirs, you stop chasing impressive and start chasing useful. Every product in our portfolio is live, in the hands of real users, doing a real job.

We build across the full stack — websites, platforms, AI, and the automation that ties them together — with a bias for the African market: local payment rails, WhatsApp-first, mobile-first, and priced for businesses here.

How we think

Four principles we don't bend on.

They're why our clients get software that works on launch day — not a project that needs another three months.

01

Shipped beats perfect

Real software in front of real users teaches more than any plan. We get to live fast, then improve with evidence.

02

Built for here

African payment rails, WhatsApp, mobile-first, offline-aware. We build for how business actually happens on the continent.

03

Own the whole stack

Design, backend, frontend, AI, deployment — one accountable team, so nothing falls through the cracks between vendors.

04

Useful over impressive

We'd rather save you ten hours a week than win a demo. If it doesn't move your business, we don't build it.
