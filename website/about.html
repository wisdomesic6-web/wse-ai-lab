Principles

Four things we don't bend on.

01

Shipped beats perfect

A live product earning feedback beats a flawless plan in a drawer. We ship in stages and improve in the open.

02

Built for here

African bandwidth, African payments, African customers. We design for the market you actually serve — Paystack, WhatsApp, low-data first.

03

Own the whole stack

Frontend, backend, data, AI, deployment. One person owns everything today, so nothing falls between the cracks.

04

Useful over impressive

We measure builds by hours saved and revenue moved, not by how good the demo looks.
